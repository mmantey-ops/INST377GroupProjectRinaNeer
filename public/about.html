Purpose

The purpose of Frutifier is to put all the fruit nutrition into one place. No more searching through different websites to find the calories, sugar, carbohydrates, protein, and fat for each fruit. Frutifier makes it easy to search for a fruit and see all the nutrition facts in a simple format. Users can also view the nutrition data in a chart and save fruits to look at later.

The Problem

Nutrition facts can be hard to compare when the information is spread across different websites. Some users may want to quickly check which fruits have more sugar and dont want to navigate to multiple sites.

Our Solution !

Frutifier makes it easy for users to search for a fruit and view its nutrition facts. All your fruit information IN ONE PLACE!
